Safety Warning

  • Change All The Factory Preset Codes.
  • Never Disclose Your Passwords To Any Unknown Person.
  • Keep The User Manual And External Adaptor In A Safe Place Incase Of Emergency (Not Inside The Safe).
  • Replace All Four Batteries Together Of Same Company With Recent Manufacturing Date Evey 12 Months.
  • Incase Of Low Battery, Use The External Adaptor Given By The Company Only.
  • Anchor The Safe Properly From Both Back And Bottom.
  • Do Not Overstuff The Safe As It May Damage The System Of The Safe.
  • Do Not Use Any Liquid While Cleaning The Keypad.
  • Do Not Keep Any Liquid Material On Top Of The Safe As It May Spill And Render The Safe Inoperable.
  • Do Not Open The Back Cover Of The Safe Or Try To Repair The Safe Yourself As It Will Void The Warranty, Rather Contact On Our Toll Free No.
